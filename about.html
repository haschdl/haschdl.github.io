About myself

One more engineer experimenting with digital art. I would love to call this a portfolio, but it would be a bit farfetched and inconsiderate with the talented artists out there. Most of the pieces here are simple exercises in various techniques and tools, therefore I rather call it a learning portfolio, the registry of my learning trajectory in digital art.

For information about my regular job in the field of business intelligence and data warehousing, please check my LinkedIn profile. When I am not busy with data or working on my creative coding projects, I might be in the kitchen cooking for my family and friends. You can check my concoctions by following my Instagram @theresilientknife

About the content
